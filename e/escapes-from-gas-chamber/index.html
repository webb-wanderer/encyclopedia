Escapes, from Gas Chamber

Numerous self-declared eyewitnesses of homicidal gas chambers have declared that they miraculously escaped from a gas chamber when they were just about to get gassed. The following individuals made such claims:

  • Regina Bialek (Auschwitz)
  • Arnold Friedman (Auschwitz)
  • Mietek Grocher (Majdanek)
  • Sofia Litwinska (Auschwitz)
  • Filip Müller (Auschwitz)
  • Moshe Peer (Bergen-Belsen)
  • Mary Seidenwurm Wrzos (Majdanek)
